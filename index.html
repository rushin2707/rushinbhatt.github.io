About Me

Hi! I'm Rushin, and my journey with AI and Machine Learning has been all about finding connections between disciplines, industries, and even art forms. As a Machine Learning intern at a fast-paced startup in Mumbai named Fynd, I built an AI-driven product catalog enrichment pipeline that showcased how large-scale machine learning and agentic AI can transform something as dynamic as retail. The beauty of this role was that I had the opportunity to work on the product side as well, directly under the CTPO, making it a perfect blend of tech and product.

At Dolcera, I explored how LLMs and other cool productivity tools can reshape the way teams collaborate at companies. In addition to these, I've gained experience across five internships in total, giving me exposure to both applied machine learning and software development in diverse settings.

I'm also a pianist, and I've completed all levels of the Trinity College of Music Classical Piano and Theory music examinations. What excites me most, though, is extending these ideas into creative spaces: I've been experimenting with how AI agents can augment music composition and performance, blending my background as a pianist with my academic interests.

I've just arrived in New York City to begin my graduate studies in Data Science at Columbia University, and I'm seeking opportunities where I can continue exploring these intersections.
